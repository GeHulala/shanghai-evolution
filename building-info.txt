上海著名建筑介绍 · Shanghai Famous Buildings Info
==================================================

1. 和平饭店 · Peace Hotel (1929)
   中文：和平饭店是外滩最著名的老酒店。1929年建成，拥有美丽的装饰艺术风格。很多国家元首曾住过这里。
   English: Peace Hotel is a famous old hotel on the Bund from 1929. It has beautiful Art Deco style. Many leaders from other countries stayed here.

2. 东方明珠塔 · Oriental Pearl Tower (1995)
   中文：东方明珠塔1995年建成，高468米。三个球体夜晚有粉色灯光，是上海标志。
   English: The Oriental Pearl Tower was built in 1995. It is 468 meters tall. It has three pink spheres that shine at night. It is the symbol of Shanghai.

3. 金茂大厦 · Jin Mao Tower (1999)
   中文：金茂大厦1999年建成，高421米。设计来自中国古塔的样子。有酒店和办公室。
   English: Jin Mao Tower was finished in 1999. It is 421 meters tall. Its look comes from old Chinese pagodas. It has a hotel and offices inside.

4. 环球金融中心 · Shanghai WFC (2008)
   中文：高492米，2008年建成。因为顶部有个洞，人们叫它"开瓶器"。
   English: Shanghai WFC is 492 meters tall. People call it the bottle opener. At the top there is a big square hole. You can see the river from floor 100.

5. 中华艺术宫 · China Art Museum (2010)
   中文：2010年世博会的中国国家馆。红色的东方之冠造型非常特别。现在展出中国艺术。
   English: This red building was the China Pavilion for the 2010 Expo. Its crown shape is very special. Now it shows Chinese art inside.

6. 上海中心大厦 · Shanghai Tower (2015)
   中文：高632米，中国第一高楼。螺旋形设计可以减少风力。有全球最快的电梯。
   English: Shanghai Tower is 632 meters tall. It is the tallest building in China. Its twisty shape makes wind go around. It has the world fastest elevator.
